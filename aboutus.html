About Sea Gate Resort

Welcome to Sea Gate Resort, your premier destination for relaxation and luxury at the heart of the Dead Sea. Opened in 2024, our resort combines breathtaking views, world-class amenities, and unmatched comfort to offer a truly unforgettable experience.

At Sea Gate Resort, we pride ourselves on providing an exceptional stay with our modern accommodations, serene spa treatments, and exquisite dining options. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring that every guest enjoys the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Whether you're looking to unwind by the pool, indulge in our rejuvenating spa services, or explore the unique attractions of the Dead Sea, Sea Gate Resort is the ideal destination for your getaway.

We invite you to discover the beauty of our resort and create lasting memories with us. Thank you for choosing Sea Gate Resort, where luxury meets tranquility.
